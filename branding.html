    Case Studies

    1. Case Study: From Local to a Global Brand – The Samagra SuperStore Journey

    Client : Samagra SuperStore (Parent Company: BBV)

    Industry : Retail & Supermarket

    The Challenge

    When BBV approached The One Solution Branding, they had a vision but no name, no identity, and no brand presence. The task? Create a brand from scratch—a name that aligns with their ethos, a visual identity that meets global standards, and a comprehensive branding strategy that would position them for long-term success.

    Our Approach

    Brand Naming & Identity:

    • We didn’t just pick a name; we crafted one with meaning. After extensive brainstorming and strategic alignment, we proposed "Samagra Super Store", which resonated with the brand’s vision, mission, and values.
    • The name was well-received and approved by BBV’s Board of Directors, marking the first major step in the brand’s journey.

    Logo & Visual Identity:

    • A brand is only as strong as its identity. We designed a modern, versatile, and globally appealing logo that embodied the essence of Samagra Super Store.
    • Our design approach ensured the logo was timeless, memorable, and scalable across all digital and print formats.

    Brand Guidelines & Material:

    • We developed a comprehensive brand guideline that outlined:
      • Color schemes & typography
      • Logo usage & adaptability
      • Marketing collateral & store branding principles
    • The branding package was built to match global standards, on par with industry giants like Apple & Airbnb—ensuring a professional and consistent brand presence across all touchpoints.

    The Impact

    • A fully realized brand identity from scratch

    • Positioned as a premium & professional retail brand

    • Strong brand consistency for long-term growth

    • Recognition as a well-structured, high-standard brand

    Final Thoughts

    This project wasn’t just about creating a brand; it was about building a legacy. From ideation to execution, The One Solution Branding transformed a vision into a reality—taking Samagra Super Store from nothing to a global-standard brand.
